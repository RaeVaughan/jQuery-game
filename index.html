Power-Up Collector

At the start of the game, you will be given a random number to guess.

By clicking one of the four power-ups below, you will add a certain number to your overall total. The number of points is hidden until you click the power-up.

To win, you must match your total with the number to guess. Go over and you lose!

When you win and lose, the game will reset with a new random number to match and new numbers assigned to each power-up.

Good luck!
