Crystal Collector

At the start of the game, you will be given a random number.

By clicking one of the four crystals below, you will add a certain number to your overall points. The number of points is hidden until you click the crystal.

To win, you must match your points with the random number. Go over and you lose!

When you win and lose, the game will reset with a new random number to match and new numbers assigned to each crystal.

Good luck!
